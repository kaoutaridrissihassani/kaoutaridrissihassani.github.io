About Me

I’m Kaoutar Idrissi Hassani, a Master’s student in Educational Technology & Pedagogical Innovation at UM5, Rabat, graduating in June 2025. I combine a strong foundation in multimedia, web technology, and computer engineering with a passion for creating innovative, tech-driven learning experiences.

My research focuses on simulation games for developing entrepreneurial skills and integrating socio-emotional learning into school curricula. I also have hands-on experience in educational projects, leadership initiatives, and cross-cultural learning, including studying Chinese in China.

Driven, curious, and solutions-oriented, I aim to transform education through technology, helping students build skills for the real world.

Experiences

Middle School Al-Khawarizmi

Student Teacher | Rabat• March 2023 – April 2023

I completed an internship as a Student Teacher at Middle School Al-Khawarizmi during March and April 2023. My role was to teach the Moroccan computer science curriculum to students, even though the school did not have computers and the subject was not officially part of their program. I introduced students to the basics of computer science, explaining the components of a computer, fundamental algorithmic principles, and essential topics such as cybersecurity and best practices for online safety. This internship allowed me to raise students’ awareness of digital literacy and provide them with a first hands-on approach to the field.

MENARA PRÉFA

Web Developer | Marrakech• AApril 2022 – May 2022

I completed a two-month internship as a Web Developer at MENARA PRÉFA in Marrakech. During this period, I developed a trainee management platform that allowed candidates to submit their CVs and information to streamline the internship application process. I worked independently on the project under the guidance of my supervisor, who provided mentorship throughout the internship. For development, I used PHP and Laravel for the backend, and JavaScript and AJAX to enhance interactivity and user experience. This experience strengthened my skills in web development and project management.

EYSI TEAM

Web Developer | Marrakech• July 2021

I completed a summer internship as a Web Developer at EYSI TEAM in Marrakech during July 2021, working remotely due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This internship helped me gain experience in developing dynamic websites using PHP and SQL for database management. During this period, I created an online magazine called "Ronbun", designed to share the latest global news across different categories. This experience enhanced my programming skills and provided a deeper understanding of remote web development challenges.

Escape Game Educatif

Ce projet consiste à concevoir un escape game éducatif immersif permettant de combiner apprentissage et divertissement. À travers des énigmes captivantes et des scénarios adaptés, les joueurs explorent des périodes historiques majeures (Grèce antique, Égypte pharaonique, époque des Lumières) tout en développant des compétences cognitives et  collaboratives. Ce jeu vise à offrir une expérience pédagogique innovante, engageante et adaptée à un public jeune, notamment dans un contexte scolaire ou ludique.
